About

Hello, my name is Arsh and I'm a master's student at Indiana University Indianapolis in applied data science. My passion is to gain insights from data and understand the world around us a bit better.

I work with variety of tools and techniques such as Python, SQL, Machine Learning, Databases and more. I love turning messy datasets to insights that are of use.

Currently, I'm working on a screen time project with the goal of gaining insight into screen time usage of people. I hope to make people reflect on their daily screen use. Besides this, I'm deepening my understanding of deep learning neural networks and hope to apply data science to real-world problems that impact lives.

Outside of data, I like to workout, spend time with family and friends, and watch tv shows, movies and some documentaries.

Feel free to reach out for collaborations, questions or opportunities!
